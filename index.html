How to play

Welcome to "Guess My Number"! This exciting game challenges you to guess a hidden number chosen by the computer. Follow this manual to learn how to play and enjoy the game.

Game Objective

The objective of "Guess My Number" is to guess the hidden number selected by the computer. The number will be between 1 and 20 inclusive.

How to Play

  1. Start the Game: When you start the game, the computer will randomly select a number between 1 and 20.
  2. Make a Guess: Enter your guess in the input field. Your guess must be a whole number between 1 and 20.
  3. Feedback: After you enter your guess, the computer will provide feedback:
    • If your guess is correct, the computer will congratulate you and the game will end.
    • If your guess is too high, the computer will inform you that the guess is too high.
    • If your guess is too low, the computer will inform you that the guess is too low.
  4. Continue Guessing: Use the feedback to adjust your next guess and continue until you guess the correct number.
  5. Winning the Game: When you guess the correct number, the computer will display a congratulatory message. You can then choose to start a new game or exit.

Rules

  • Guess Range: You must guess a number between 1 and 20. Guesses outside this range are invalid.
  • Single Input: Enter only one number at a time in the input field.
  • Continuous Play: The game continues until you guess the correct number. There is no limit to the number of guesses you can make.

Tips for Success

Start in the Middle: A good initial guess is 10, which is in the middle of the range. This can help you quickly determine whether the hidden number is higher or lower.

Use Feedback Wisely: Adjust your guesses based on whether the feedback indicates your guess was too high or too low.

Narrow Down: Each time you receive feedback, you can halve the range of possible numbers, making it easier to zero in on the correct guess.

Example Game

  1. The computer selects a hidden number (e.g., 14).
  2. You guess 10.
    • The computer responds, "Too low!"
  3. You guess 15.
    • The computer responds, "Too high!"
  4. You guess 13.
    • The computer responds, "Too low!"
  5. You guess 14.
    • The computer responds, "Congratulations! You guessed the correct number!"

Troubleshooting

  • Invalid Input: If you enter a number outside the range of 1 to 20, the computer will prompt you to enter a valid number within the range.
  • Non-Numeric Input: If you enter non-numeric characters, the computer will prompt you to enter a valid number.

Conclusion

"Guess My Number" is a simple yet engaging game that sharpens your number-guessing skills. Enjoy the challenge, use logical reasoning, and have fun guessing!

Happy playing!
